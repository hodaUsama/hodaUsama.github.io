Q: In a neighborhood of 9 houses, 8 are priced around $300,000–$350,000, but 1 is a mansion worth $3.5 million. Which measure of central tendency would best describe the typical house price?

💡 Show Answer

✅ Median — it’s the most robust to outliers like the mansion, unlike the mean which would be skewed upward.

Bonus: What is the only measure of central tendency that can be used for categorical (non-numerical) data?

💡 Show Answer

✅ Mode — it identifies the most frequent category, like the most common favorite color or pet type.
